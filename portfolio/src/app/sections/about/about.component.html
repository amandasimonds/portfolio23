About Me

Hello! My name is Amanda and I love to create beautiful digital experiences.

My interest in web development started back in 2007, when I was creating custom MySpace and blog layouts with HTML/CSS. After spending some time as a design student and art teacher, I decided to try combining my visual skills with web development, and turns out, that's a really good combo!

With a visually detail oriented, and problem solving mind, I am passionate about creating efficieny in design-development processes that result in exceptional user experiences. In my current role, I've led the fight for consistent UI with a well-documented component library, and contribution process.
